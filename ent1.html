Brigiding Crowned First as “Slayasian Royale” Superstar in Drag Race Philippines Spin-Off

The stage shimmered with glitter, glamour, and gratified emotion showed by Brigiding “Gigi” Archeta, or simply Brigiding – The first officially crowned winner of Drag Race Philippines: Slayasian Royale, a monumental spin-off celebrating the Asian drag artistry.


12 queens gathered from across Asia and other Asian communities to complete the title of “Slayasian Royale Drag Race Superstar”. Hosted by Paulo Ballesteros, they featured drag queens from Philippines alongside Australia, Belgium, Canada, France, Thailand, United Kingdom, and United States. Brigiding managed to triumph over the 11 other queens and brought victory after weeks of fierce runway looks, outstanding lipsync performances, and heartfelt stories of identity and resilience.


“To everyone reading this: everything happens for a reason. Keep reaching for your dreams and hold on to it because your time will come and dreams don’t expire.” – Brigiding’s caption on her Instagram post. The season, which premiered in August 13, 2025 on WOW Presents Plus and HBO Go Philippines, quickly became one of the most-watched Drag Race spin-offs worldwide. Fans praised the production for its dazzling and authentic representation of Asian Drag Culture.

K-Drama Heartthrob Hyun Bin Reflects first to the Philippines.

Hyun Bin is one of the rising actors internationally. He is loved by many Filipinos because of his looks and wonderful dramas. Over the years, Hyun Bin’s roles in various dramas helped him gain multiple Filipino fans.

His dramas quickly became favorites among Filipino viewers, and many look forward to any further projects he does. In an interview, Hyun Bin chatted about his first trip to the Philippines. He said he was moved by how hospitable and welcoming Filipinos were. Fans met him at the airport with smiles, banners, and small gifts, which made him feel deeply grateful. He also said their kind souls made the trip meaningful to him. He was able to visit a few places in the city even though his schedule was hectic. He also tried some Filipino dishes during his stay and conveyed that everyone he met was very friendly.


Hyun Bin said he wishes to return one day, not just for work but also to explore more beaches and learn about the country’s culture. Filipino fans continue to support his projects, revealing how strong their connection with him has become. A lot of people admire him because of his humbleness and respect for every person he encounters with especially his beloved fans, the Filipinos, despite what he has achieved in life. His career overseas is still thriving, and Filipinos are excited to see his future roles. Fans also wish he could visit the Philippines again and stay longer next time.
